Biography

Bob's real name was "Robert Nesta Marley". Bob was born Feb 6 1945, and Died May 11 1981, at the age of 36. Bob was born in Nine Mile Jamaica, and began his professional career in 1963. Bob was not only a Legend, but that was also the name of his greatest hits album, released in 19874, which became the best-selling raggae album of all time. - Sourced at Wikipedia.org
